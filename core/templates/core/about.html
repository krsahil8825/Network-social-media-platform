About Network

Network is a simple social media website where users can post text-based content, share their thoughts, comment on other posts, and edit or manage their own posts. The platform is designed to encourage open communication and meaningful interactions within a clean, distraction-free environment.

Our goal is to create a lightweight and easy-to-use platform focused purely on text and ideas — no unnecessary noise, just genuine conversations.

Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. Your personal data such as username and email are stored securely and never shared with third parties. All user-generated content remains your property.

We only collect information required to keep your account functional — such as login credentials and post activity. Cookies may be used to maintain login sessions and improve your browsing experience.

By using Network, you consent to our privacy practices as described here.

Terms & Conditions

By using Network, you agree to:

  • Post only respectful and appropriate content.
  • Refrain from harassment, hate speech, or illegal activity.
  • Understand that administrators may remove content that violates our policies.
  • Use the platform responsibly and respect the opinions of others.

Violation of these terms may result in account suspension or permanent removal from the platform.
